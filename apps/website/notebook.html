Why your decisions need a record

Patent law has always required that inventors document when and how they conceived their ideas. In the age of AI-assisted development, that documentation is more important than ever — because the line between "your idea" and "the AI's idea" needs to be clear.

The challenge: most developers make their most important creative decisions in the moment — and forget them by the time they talk to an attorney. A choice you made at 2pm on a Tuesday becomes impossible to reconstruct six months later.

The Inventor's Notebook solves this by making it fast and natural to capture those moments when they happen.
